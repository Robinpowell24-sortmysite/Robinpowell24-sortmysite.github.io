Why do a site usability and design audit?

Does your site have traffic but lacks click-through rates? I will help you identify issues and also give you fast, actionable ways to fix and improve your website.

Low click-through rates

Your site confuses your users and they don't know what to do or where to go. Your nav might be unintuitive or your layout doesn't guide your customers to click through.

Users don't know what you do

Your text or graphics tell a differentsto ry to each other. Your colours say one thing but your brand message says something else. Your font says playful but your content says serious.

Site is too slow

Users expect a site to load in less than 2 seconds, 3 seconds if they are patient. Any more than that and they may abandon your page.

Conflicting messages

Different style CTA's. A mix of image styles and too many different text styles and fonts: all these will lead to your users not trusting your site.

Web page accessibility issues

You're not optimised for screen readers. Your site is not accessible to all users. Colours don't pass standards, and keyboard users get stuck. You need to address all of these and more.

Numerous other reasons

There are lots of other reasons to do a usability and design audit for your site. If you want increased click-through rates book a call now.
